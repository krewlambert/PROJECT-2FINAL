About Chiquimamani-Condori

background visuals from The Total Angelica - Elysia Crampton
After critically acclaimed records as Elysia Crampton or E+E, Chuquimamani-Condori takes their Aymara name -
part of the Great Pakajaqi nation, currently occupied by the states of Bolivia, Peru, Chile and Argentina -
reclaiming their intrinsic identity and historical and cultural baggage. A multidisciplinary artist based in California,
they develop an ongoing process of reconfiguration around the legacy of cumbia, tarqueada and other sounds and dances from the Andes,
both devotional and speculative - a sound that projects possible futures of a tradition lived in the present’s hyperactive continuum.
Los Thutanaka is a collaborative project between Chiquimamani & their brother, Joshua Chiquima Crampton. The debut album, Los Thutanaka,
was awarded Album of the Year 2025 by Pitchfork.

This website is a listening guide to their work, which spans over many platforms and aliases. This is not a comprehensive catalog,
but rather a curated selection of my personal favorite releases. The shuffle feature allows you to explore their discography in
a randomized order. Sometimes releases are hidden behind another, and I encourage you to seek out the ones that are harder to get to.
Enjoy!
